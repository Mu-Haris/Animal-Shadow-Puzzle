Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Kinex Apps ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Animal Shadow Puzzle mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We take privacy very seriously and minimize data collection:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information from users.
  • No Account Registration: The App does not require users to create an account or log in.
  • Device Identifiers: If you consent to tracking (via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt), an advertising identifier (IDFA) may be used by our advertising partners to serve personalized ads. You can opt out at any time through your device settings.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (such as which levels are played most) to improve the App. This data cannot identify individual users.

2. Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

  • Banner ads are shown on the home and category screens.
  • Interstitial ads may be shown between game rounds.
  • You can remove all ads by purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase ($1.99).
  • If you decline tracking, ads will still be shown but will not be personalized.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers a non-consumable in-app purchase to remove advertisements. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

4. Data Storage

Game settings (volume preferences, purchase status) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data is not transmitted to any server.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — for processing in-app purchases. Privacy Policy

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of ad personalization via iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking.
  • Delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the App.
  • Request information about data practices by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
