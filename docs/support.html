Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my "Remove Ads" purchase?

Open the Settings (gear icon) in the app and tap "Restore." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Your purchase will be restored automatically.

The app is crashing or not loading properly.

Try these steps:

  • Make sure your device is running the latest version of iOS.
  • Close and reopen the app.
  • Restart your device.
  • Delete and reinstall the app (your purchase will be restored).

How do I remove ads?

You can remove all advertisements by purchasing "Remove Ads" for $1.99. This can be done through the promo popup that appears when you open the app, or through the Settings panel (gear icon) by tapping the "Remove Ads" button.

Is this app safe for my child?

Yes! Animal Shadow Puzzle is designed specifically for young children (kindergartners). We do not collect personal information, require account creation, or include any inappropriate content. Ads shown comply with child-directed advertising policies. You can also remove all ads with a one-time purchase.

What age group is this app for?

Animal Shadow Puzzle is designed for children ages 2-6, though children of all ages (and adults!) can enjoy it. The three difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard) allow the game to grow with your child.

Can I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow Apple's standard refund process.

How do I adjust volume?

Tap the gear icon on any screen to open Settings. You can adjust Music and Sound FX volume independently using the caterpillar sliders.
